Sean Wilt

My name is Sean Wilt, and I enjoy playing video games (my favorite would have to be the Legend of Zelda : Majora's Mask), learning programming (I'm currently learning C#,Java,and improving on my HTML & CSS skills.), and hanging out with friends in my spare time. If I had to pick a programming language that I enjoy the most it would have to be PHP, I don't know why it just really clicked with me and I'm very talented when using it. Some more things about me are that my favorite food is Pizza (it's just so good.).

Ever since my junior year of high school I have been studying computer programming, the inital thing that drew me to the field is that I wanted to make my own video game, but as I learned more I started becoming more interested in just the general development of software and web pages. My college experience so far has been... interesting, but I'm glad that I'm currently pursuing my degree rather than just working all the time.

The web plays such a massive role in our everyday lives for the simple fact that it is so intergrated it used to be that you could have a business and to get your word out all you needed were some business cards, maybe an ad in the paper, and if you were lucky enough a billboard, but now adays if you don't have some kind of digital advertising you're business is hurting, in addition to that it's made it so much easier to share information with people; want to share that awesome chicken noodle soup recipie with your family?, well then share it with them on Facebook and boom! they can all see it almost instantaniously.
